ABOUT US

THE PROJECT

POLYCHROMES

"A new era has just begun will you be part of it ? The age of the internet is soon over and a new world is coming : THE METAVERSE !

"Our planet is full of different resources, cultures and ethnicities. We are all Humans, but we are not all the same: we have our own customs and traditions, depending on our origins for thousands of years ! Every human being belongs to a community with its own values, trials and history. Our objective and our duty will be to preserve the memory of our ancestors, which tends to be erased over the centuries. For this, we will gather a community of experts and historians specialized in the knowledge of each community: European, African, Asian."

The POLYCHROMES are Men and Women under an ultra realistic 3D representation, which represents each one their ethnicity and their culture among our great diversity. We will represent and reunite our great peoples. Art is the way to reflect the deep feelings we have in our hearts through creativity, we will be able to tell you a story, YOUR story!
